Vision

We want Screamin' Coffee Shop to be renowned as one of the best places in town to get amazing coffee and a complementary muffin.

Mission

Having a unique and high-quality coffee shop is the mission of Screamin' Coffee Shop. Not just that, we also want our customers to be happy and enjoy our coffee and other drinks, as well.
C U S T O M E R

R E L A T I O N S H I P

The first thing that we do to build our relationship with customer is we do a promotion and discounts which is we will do a promotion on every holiday season, public holiday and every festival thus we will give a 10% discounts for the third cup of beverages or coffee to attract loyal customer. Next, we will reach people around us about the deliciousness and the quality ingredients of the coffee and beverages that we offer. Moreover, to attract the customer, we always give a good services such as keep a friendly customer services, gives a quick service and always smile to welcome the customer and the last one is we conduct a survey for feedback of the quality and service that we provided to them thus we also welcome a suggestion and recommendation about our coffee and beverages.

C H A N N E L S

The channel that our business choose to reach our customer is direct service. Customer can just come to our café and can have their coffee directly in our café without have to order on online platform. Next, we also use the social media such as Instagram, Whatsapp, Twitter and Tiktok. We can keep updating the customer about our product on social media, thus they can also direct message us on this platform for order for pickup without have to wait or dine at our café. Furthermore, we use the advertising such as poster and flyers to promote our product and to reach customer around us quickly and the last channel that we use is online delivery platform which is customer can order our coffee and beverage through food panda delivery platform.


C U S T O M E R S E G M E N T S

For our business, our consumer segment will be customer from all range of age such as children, teenager, student, working people, general public and internet user because all range of age loves coffee, and for child and for those who are not into coffee, they can drink our beverages that are not coffee based. Not only that, our consumer segment also will be customer from all races and from any level of income, since our coffee and beverages are inexpensive enough to drink.

The first one is we provide only the most natural of products. Our coffee is grown by organic farmers so there are no chemicals used in the process of coffee beans.

2.Pricing

Another reason why some customers would choose to purchase coffee from Screamin Coffee is because of the pricing itself. The price of the beverage in Screamin Coffee is affordable which it is rather cheap and nice.

3.Promotion

Another factor that influences a customer in paying more for coffee at Screamin' Coffee rather than a local coffee shop is because of the variety of promotions which are available; varying from the promotional food and beverages or limited edition whole beans.

4.Packaging

As for some customers the packaging of the products attracts them to purchase at the Screamin Coffee. No doubt that a product with a very attractive feature from its packaging will get the attention of customers and in this case products of Screamin Coffee has that attribute of having nicely packaged products.
