LEARN HOW TO MAKE

OUR SIGNATURE COFFEE

"When you feel depresso,
Have a little espresso"

Step 1


The standard ratio is approximately 1 or 2 tablespoons of coffee. But you can adjust the amount of coffee powder to your liking.

Step 2


The standard sugar is 1 or 2 tablespoons of sugar. You can adjust the amount of sugar in your coffee. Some individuals prefer their coffee to be sweet, while others prefer it to be bitter.

Step 3


After that, pour hot water into your cup. It's important not to overlook the water you use to brew the coffee.

Step 4


Stir the coffee. mix your coffee in 30 to 1 minute until well blended.

Step 5


Put some ice. if you want to make ice coffee, you can put ice but if you want hot coffee, you don't have to put any.

Step 6


After you put some ice in your coffee, in a gentle but quick stream, pour the milk in. This will cool down the coffee/milk mixture, it will form a layer of foam at the top of the glass.
